Joseph Pellegrino

lead me to a research position, where I can add to the pool of knowledge in my
field. As far as research goes, I am quite interested in working with Artificial

of human emotion.
				
Along with my aforementioned passion for Computer Science lies a multitude of
other interests. I am quite the fan of writing, whether it be prose or poetry;
I am drawn to anything that allows me to utilize my creativity. This yearning
for expression also draws me towards Photoshop and other image-editing programs.
My final passion is for starting projects. I enjoy taking on a task and seeing
it through to the end, which comes in handy when designing software.
